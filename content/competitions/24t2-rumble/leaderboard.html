About the Leaderboard

The leaderboard shows the scores of all participants who have scored a non-zero number of points. It may be behind the actual scores by up to 20 seconds, and will not refresh unless you reload the page.

If two participants have the same total score, the participant who most recently made a submission which strictly increased their total score has a larger rank.

* If a user's rank is an asterisk they were ineligible for prizes.
